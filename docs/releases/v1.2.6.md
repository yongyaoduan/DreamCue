# DreamCue v1.2.6

## Highlights

- Adds tenant-scoped Firebase sync at `users/{uid}/memos` for cue order, pinned state, restore, completion, and deletion tombstones.
- Adds pinned cues above regular cues with `display_order`, persistent drag ordering, archive-wide search, and restore-to-today behavior.
- Sends individual daily notifications for pinned active cues and one `DreamCue` summary notification for all active cues.
- Refines Android with 0.5-second long-press drag ordering, a wheel-style reminder picker, a cleaner pinned row treatment, and notification-only reminders.
- Refines macOS with the `DreamCue` app name, time-based greetings, full-row navigation hit targets, Return-to-save editing, compact Account and Rhythm layouts, and standard-control reminder time selection.
